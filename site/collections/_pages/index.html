Hi, I'm Eleanor, sometimes known as Lithium. I am a community care worker offering emotional, spiritual, and logistical support to people with or without disabilities. I help clients utilize tools like acceptance, proactive care, and mindfulness practices that can help grow capacity for resilience in a harsh world. Creating a non-judgemental, collaborative environment from which to navigate medical systems and social stigmas associated with chronic illness and disability is essential to developing acceptance, perspective, and healing.


You can read more about me here.
