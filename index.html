Disability Mentorship
& Coaching

Hi, I'm Eleanor, sometimes known as Lithium. I am a community care worker offering emotional, spiritual, and logistical support to people with or without disabilities. I help clients develop their own systems for growth to help with capacity for resilience in a harsh world. Creating a non-judgemental, collaborative environment from which to navigate medical systems and social stigmas associated with chronic illness and disability is essential to developing acceptance, perspective, and healing.
